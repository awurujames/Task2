About 'WeFive'

The 'We Five' is an ochestra musical group and its one of the best orchestra group in the world based in liverpool in the United Kindom. The group consist of eighteen female members who are gifted in music. The group was carefully selected from Got Tallents hunt among different nationalities around the world. These tallents were amazing in thier respective Got Tallent performances. They were selected so as to bring the best out of ochestra music.

The 'We Five' has been in existence since November 1991 and was founded by the legendry Micheal Jackson. Jackson had a great love for ochestra music and decided to bring these tallents together, since he is not gifted in that genre of music.

Performances

Ever since the inception of this musical group, They have continualy done great musical diplays locally and internationally. Having the full support of Micheal Jackson, the group has evolved over the years and have played in world stages. they have played in big occation with dignetries and has won the heart of so many.

So far, so good, the ochestra group has won so many awards running from the 'Best Musical Group in the Untied Kindom', 'Best ochestra music of the year', "Best Musical Instrumentals in the world", the Grammy award etc.
